# ADR 0055: Admin Authoring Form Guidance

## Context

The admin authoring screens are used by Korean operators, executives, and future content managers who may not be familiar with the internal CMS model. News/video and category content forms were functional, but the fields were visually grouped too lightly, making it harder to understand the recommended input order.

## Decision

Add Korean section guidance to authoring forms.

- News/video drafts are grouped into basic classification, body copy, and media/source sections.
- Category content drafts distinguish category choice, public content, and shared operations information.
- Guidance explains where each field appears or how it affects review and publication.

## Consequences

The admin experience becomes easier for non-developer operators without changing validation, storage shape, or publication workflow.
